Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Welcome to SalonPro. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how SalonPro ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, protects, and handles your information when you use our web application located at complete-salon-management.vercel.app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information strictly necessary to provide and optimize our B2B salon management platform. This includes:

  • Account Credentials: User ID, name, email address, password hash, and salon name provided during registration.
  • Salon Operational Data: Customer registries, appointment logs, service histories, bills, staff check-in details, and commission structures.
  • Contact Information: Phone numbers and email addresses of salon personnel and clients, used exclusively for notifications and invoice dispatching.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is processed strictly for the operational needs of your business, including:

  • Creating and securing your administrative and staff accounts.
  • Managing client bookings, appointments, and billing profiles.
  • Calculating staff attendance, commissions, and operational performance.
  • Sending transaction confirmation emails, password reset codes, and critical service notifications.

3. Data Protection and Non-Disclosure

We hold our data security to a high standard. We guarantee that:

  • No Selling of Data: We do not sell, rent, trade, or distribute your personal or business data to third-party advertisers or marketers.
  • Strict Access Controls: Your database documents are isolated per tenant workspace and secured via strict JWT token authentication.
  • Encryption Standards: All transit data is protected via HTTPS (SSL/TLS). Cryptographic hashing (bcryptjs) is applied to all user passwords before database write operations.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain operational data as long as your subscription or user account remains active. You may request permanent deletion of your account and all associated operational records at any time by contacting our support team. Upon verification, the requested records will be permanently purged from our active database within 30 days.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@salonpro.com.
